Our Mission

Our Vision

To be global leaders in Robotics and automation research.

Senior-Junior Teaching

We freely approach seniors and mentors to discuss how we can improve our robots and creations. Through their encouragement and constructive criticism, we improve and grow together as a club, building trust and forming positive relationships amongst ourselves.

Passion for Robotics

Our passion for Robotics is what fuels us to strive for greater heights! When competitions draw near (before COVID-19), we return to our room during the holidays or after school in preparation for upcoming competitions or projects!

Creative Projects

Besides preparing for competitions, we also work on our own projects to express our creativity through unique inventions and innovations.
